MAT-DP (Material Demand Projections) is a model developed by the University of Cambridge. MAT-DP compares the material implications of changing energy and transport systems over time.
Conditions of use

Select the country for which the climate data will be displayed.
Select the scenario, which corresponds to a certain energy generation strategy.
